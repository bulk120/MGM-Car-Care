Our Car Care & Detailing Services

At MGM Car Care, we offer a comprehensive range of professional car cleaning and detailing services to keep your vehicle looking its best. Our skilled technicians use premium products and advanced techniques to deliver exceptional results.

Basic Car Care Services

Car Wash (RO Water)

Gentle wash using purified RO water to prevent water spots and protect your car's paint.

Organic Foam Wash

Deep cleaning with organic foam that lifts dirt without damaging the paint surface.

Automatic Undercohesion Spa

Advanced undercarriage cleaning to remove dirt, salt, and grime that can cause rust.

Interior Vacuum Cleaner

Thorough interior vacuuming of seats, carpets, and floor mats.

Dash Board Polish

Professional dashboard cleaning and polishing to restore shine and protect from UV damage.

Engine Room Polish

Engine bay cleaning and polishing for a cleaner, more efficient engine compartment.

Tyre Polish

Tire cleaning and shining to give your wheels a fresh, new look.

Alloy Wheel Cleaning

Specialized cleaning for alloy wheels to remove brake dust and restore shine.

Manual Wax Polish

Hand-applied wax polish for superior shine and protection.

Air Freshener

Premium air fresheners to keep your car smelling fresh and clean.

Specialized Services

Ozone Therapy Treatment

Eliminates bacteria, viruses, mold, and odors from your car's interior using advanced ozone technology.

AC Gas Top-Up

Professional AC gas refill to ensure optimal cooling performance.

AC Vent Cleaning

Deep cleaning of AC vents to remove dust, allergens, and improve air quality.

Rat Spray

Protective spray to prevent rodents from damaging your car's wiring and interior.

Accessories Installation

Professional installation of car accessories and customization options.

Premium Protection Services

Ceramic Coating

Advanced nanotechnology coating that provides long-lasting protection against UV rays, chemicals, and minor scratches.

Graphene Coating

The latest in paint protection technology offering superior hardness, heat resistance, and hydrophobic properties.

Teflon Coating

High-quality Teflon coating for excellent shine and protection against environmental contaminants.

Headlight Polishing

Restore cloudy or yellowed headlights to like-new condition for improved visibility and appearance.

Rain Repellent Treatment

Hydrophobic treatment for windshields and windows that causes rain to bead up and roll off.

Salt Water Removal

Specialized treatment to remove salt deposits from coastal driving that can cause corrosion.

Note: All our services come with a satisfaction guarantee. Our trained technicians use only premium products and follow industry best practices to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible.
