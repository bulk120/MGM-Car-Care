Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 30, 2025

At MGM Car Care, we understand that sometimes plans change. This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations, rescheduling, and refunds for our services. By booking an appointment with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Appointment Cancellation by Customer

  1. Cancellation Timeframe:
    • More than 6 hours before appointment: Free cancellation with no charges
    • Between 3-6 hours before appointment: 25% cancellation fee of the scheduled service cost
    • Less than 3 hours before appointment or no-show: 50% cancellation fee of the scheduled service cost
  2. How to Cancel: Cancellations must be made by:
    • Calling us at 81110 08061, 81110 08062, or 81110 08063
    • Sending a WhatsApp message to 81110 08061
    • Emailing us at mgmcarcarerjpm@gmail.com

    Voice messages and emails are considered received only when we acknowledge them.

  3. Emergency Cancellations: In case of genuine emergencies (medical emergencies, accidents, etc.), cancellation fees may be waived at our discretion upon provision of appropriate documentation.

2. Appointment Rescheduling

  1. Rescheduling Timeframe: You may reschedule your appointment without penalty if done at least 6 hours before the scheduled time.
  2. Late Rescheduling: Rescheduling requests made less than 6 hours before the appointment may be subject to availability and may incur a rescheduling fee of 10% of the service cost.
  3. Multiple Rescheduling: Repeated rescheduling of appointments may result in restrictions on future bookings.

3. Cancellation by MGM Car Care

  1. Reasons for Cancellation: We reserve the right to cancel appointments due to:
    • Unforeseen circumstances (power outages, equipment failure, etc.)
    • Weather conditions that make service unsafe or impossible
    • Staff emergencies or unavailability
    • If the vehicle condition poses a safety risk
    • Suspected fraudulent activity
  2. Notification: We will make every effort to notify you as soon as possible if we need to cancel your appointment.
  3. Compensation: If we cancel your appointment, we will offer to reschedule at your earliest convenience or provide a full refund if rescheduling is not possible.

4. Refund Policy

  1. Service Dissatisfaction: If you are not satisfied with our service, please notify us immediately before leaving our facility. We will make reasonable efforts to address your concerns. If we are unable to resolve the issue to your satisfaction, we may offer a partial or full refund at our discretion.
  2. Refund Processing:
    • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
    • Refunds will be issued using the original payment method whenever possible
    • Cash payments will be refunded in cash
    • Card payments will be refunded to the original card
    • UPI payments will be refunded to the original UPI ID
  3. Non-Refundable Services: The following are generally non-refundable:
    • Services already rendered and completed
    • Special order products or materials
    • Discounts or promotional offers (if terms are violated)

5. Package and Prepaid Services

Service Type Cancellation Terms Refund Policy
Single Service Appointments As per standard cancellation policy Full refund if cancelled >6 hours in advance
Service Packages (Multiple visits) 20% cancellation fee for unused services Pro-rated refund for unused services minus cancellation fee
Prepaid Maintenance Plans 30-day written notice required Pro-rated refund minus 25% administrative fee
Special Offers/Promotions Subject to offer-specific terms As per promotional terms

6. Special Circumstances

  1. Vehicle Unavailability: If your vehicle becomes unavailable due to accidents, repairs, or other reasons, please notify us immediately. We will work with you to reschedule or provide appropriate refunds.
  2. Quality Issues: If you believe our service did not meet the promised quality standards, please contact us within 48 hours of service completion. We will inspect the vehicle and take appropriate corrective action.
  3. Damages: In the rare event that damage occurs to your vehicle during service, please report it immediately. We will investigate and take appropriate responsibility as per our liability policy.

7. How to Request Cancellation or Refund

To request a cancellation or refund:

  1. Contact Information:
    • Phone: 81110 08061, 81110 08062, or 81110 08063
    • WhatsApp: 81110 08061
    • Email: mgmcarcarerjpm@gmail.com
    • In Person: Visit our facility during business hours
  2. Information Required: When contacting us, please provide:
    • Your name and contact information
    • Vehicle registration number
    • Scheduled appointment date and time
    • Service booked
    • Reason for cancellation/refund request
    • Payment receipt or reference number
  3. Processing Time: Refund requests will be processed within 3-5 business days of approval. Actual refund receipt may take 7-10 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Cancellation and Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the modified policy.
